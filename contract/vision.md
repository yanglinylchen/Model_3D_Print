# Vision

Build a macOS portable desktop voxel modeling app for 3D-printable block creations. The app should feel approachable for Taiwan Traditional Chinese children's education while remaining useful for general 3D-print hobbyists. The modeling experience should feel similar to Minecraft building, but the output must be a printable STL mesh with real geometry: rounded block edges, visible seams, and material-specific relief textures.

Status: confirmed for initial design and contract check.
